Customer Support Assistant for an online retailer.

The system uses OpenAI GPT-4o to classify incoming tickets, summarize customer requests and draft suggested replies for the support team.

Inputs can include customer names, email addresses, order details and complaint descriptions. The assistant does not make final decisions by itself. A support lead reviews a sample of outputs every week and escalates any unsafe suggestion.

Conversation logs are retained for 30 days in an EU region for troubleshooting and service quality review.

The system is used for support triage, classification and response drafting, not for credit scoring, recruitment or biometric identification.
