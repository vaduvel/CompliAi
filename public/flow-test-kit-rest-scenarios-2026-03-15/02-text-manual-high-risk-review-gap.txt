Recruitment Screening Assistant for a high-volume employer.

The system scores CVs, ranks candidates automatically and recommends which applicants should be rejected before interview.

Inputs include CV text, work history, education, skills, location and other personal data. The current operational process does not require human review before low-ranked candidates are filtered out.

Candidate profiles and scoring outputs are stored for 365 days and are available to teams outside the EU through a US-hosted workspace.

The workflow is described internally as automated candidate scoring and prioritization.
